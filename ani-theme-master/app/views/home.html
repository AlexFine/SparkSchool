Vision

Spark Charter School’s progressive approach to learning addresses how a child grows intellectually, creatively, emotionally, socially, morally, and physically. We are committed to creating a school that is racially, ethnically, and culturally diverse, reflecting the diversity of our community, that seeks to engage parents through a commitment to family participation. We seek to nurture each student’s sense of personal integrity, care for others, and respect for individuality. Spark Charter School’s vigorous, theme-based academic program prepares students for a lifetime of learning and community involvement.

About Us

Founded in 2014, Spark Charter School is an innovative, K-8 public charter school with a mission to ignite a passion for learning and to inspire our students to become self-motivated, enthusiastic participants in their education. Spark’s goal is to be at the forefront of educational excellence by integrating within our teaching research that it is possible to effectively engage learners through the identification and strengthening of the sensory system in order to develop cognitive capacities. We believe in leveraging what we know about neuroplasticity to help students realize their potential.

Founded For Families - Founded By Families

In 2012, several parents in Sunnyvale came together around the idea that kids should be having fun when they are learning. They believed that students should be fully engaged and active in the learning process--something that was missing at their current school. It was through sheer grit and determination that Spark Charter School opened in 2015 to establish the only K-8 public school in Sunnyvale. As Spark grows, so does its commitment and effort to establish on of the most innovative, technology infused learning environments in the South Bay. Come and experience the difference--help your child to spark their love for learning by being a part of the Spark Charter School community.
